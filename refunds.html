Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

This policy applies to the paid "Parivar Shield" subscription offered through the Digital Guardian app by Digital Guardian ("we", "us").

1. Cancellation

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time — from within the Digital Guardian app, or directly from the UPI Autopay mandate in your UPI app (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.), or by emailing neerajhcumca@gmail.com.
  • On cancellation, automatic renewal stops and you will not be charged again.
  • Your paid features remain active until the end of the current paid period; access is not cut off immediately on cancellation.

2. Refunds

  • Subscription fees are charged in advance for the chosen period (monthly or yearly) and are generally non-refundable for the period already started, because access to the service is provided immediately.
  • Billing errors / duplicate charges: if you are charged in error, or charged more than once for the same period, we will refund the incorrect amount in full. Email neerajhcumca@gmail.com within 7 days of the charge with your registered mobile number and the transaction reference.
  • Accidental renewal: if an auto-renewal was charged and you contact us within 48 hours of that charge without having materially used the paid features in the new period, we will, at our discretion, cancel and refund that renewal.
  • Approved refunds are processed back to the original UPI account / payment method within 5–7 business days.

3. Free plan

Personal scam protection is free and involves no charge, so no refund is applicable to it.

4. How to request a refund

Email neerajhcumca@gmail.com from your registered email or include your registered mobile number, the transaction reference/UTR, and a short description. We aim to respond within 2 business days.

5. Contact

For any question about this policy, see our Contact Us page.
