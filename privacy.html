Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Digital Guardian ("we", "us") handles your information in the Digital Guardian app. Protecting privacy is core to our product: wherever possible, scam analysis happens on your device and we keep the personal data we hold to a minimum.

1. Information we collect

  • Account details: your mobile number (for sign-in via phone verification) and the display name and preferences you set.
  • Identity verification: we use Aadhaar OTP verification to confirm you are a real person. We store only a verified-flag and coarse, non-identifying attributes (an age band and gender) used to tailor scam guidance. We do not store your full Aadhaar number.
  • Safety signals: when the app detects a serious scam, it may record a minimal incident record (type, risk level, time) to compute your safety score and alert your chosen family guardians.
  • Optional location: if you consent, a coarse district/state (never precise GPS coordinates) to show region-specific scam awareness.
  • Device & diagnostics: a push-notification token and basic, anonymised usage events to operate and improve the service.

2. What stays on your device

Your SMS and chat message content is scanned on your device. The text of your messages is not sent to or stored on our servers. When you explicitly ask the app to check a specific message or call, only that snippet is sent for AI analysis and is not retained after the check.

3. How we use information

  • To detect and warn you about scams and to coach you during a suspicious call or message.
  • To alert family members you have added, when you face a serious scam (the "Parivar Shield" family layer), only if you have enabled sharing.
  • To verify identity, prevent abuse, and keep the community of users genuine.
  • To process subscriptions and provide support.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with:

  • Service providers who help us run the app — our payment partner (for subscription billing), cloud hosting, identity-verification and AI providers — bound to process data only on our instructions.
  • Family members you choose, who receive safety alerts about you when you enable sharing.
  • Authorities, where required by law.

5. Payments

Subscription payments are processed by our authorised payment partner. We do not collect or store your card or UPI credentials; those are handled directly by the payment partner under their own security and privacy terms.

6. Data retention

We keep account and minimal safety data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time, after which we delete or anonymise your personal data except where we must retain records to meet legal obligations.

7. Your rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal data, withdraw consent (e.g., location or family sharing), and contact us with any privacy concern at neerajhcumca@gmail.com.

8. Children

Digital Guardian is intended for adults. The optional "Kids Mode" provides safety guidance and never collects a child's personal data on our servers.

9. Changes

We may update this policy and will post the revised version here with a new "last updated" date.

10. Contact

Questions? See our Contact Us page or email neerajhcumca@gmail.com.
