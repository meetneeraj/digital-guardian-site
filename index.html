What Digital Guardian does

AI message & link shield

Checks SMS, WhatsApp and links for scam intent, not just keywords — flagging KYC fraud, OTP theft, lottery and digital-arrest scams instantly.

Live scam call coach

Listens (on the device) during a suspicious call and warns the user aloud in their language if a scammer is at work.

UPI & payment firewall

Adds a "stop and think" moment before a payment during an active scam window, and explains the risk.

Parivar Shield — family circle

The earning adult can watch over their parents: real-time alerts when a loved one faces a serious scam, and recovery help.

Vernacular by design

Works in English, Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu and Marathi — every alert in the user's own language.

Privacy-first

Message scanning happens on the device; we never store your message text or your full Aadhaar number. See our Privacy Policy.
